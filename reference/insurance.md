# US Health Insurance Data

A dataset with 1338 observations on health insurance charges and
demographic factors.

## Usage

``` r
insurance
```

## Format

A data frame with 1338 rows and 7 variables:

- age:

  age of primary beneficiary in years

- sex:

  insurance contractor sex, male/female

- bmi:

  body mass index

- children:

  number of dependents

- smoker:

  smoker/non-smoker

- region:

  beneficiary's region of US

- charges:

  individual medical costs billed by health insurance
